Proposal 3: AI-Powered True Cost Flight Comparison System
1.
Problem Statement
When planning personal travel, many people use flight comparison platforms to search for cheap tickets. However, these platforms often show only the basic fare of Low-Cost Carriers (LCCs). The final price may become much higher after adding fees for checked baggage, carry-on upgrades, seat selection, meals, and payment processing. Users often need to visit different airline websites to calculate the real cost by themselves, which is time-consuming and confusing. In some cases, an LCC ticket may even become more expensive than a full-service airline after all add-on fees are included. Therefore, a clearer system is needed to help users compare the true travel cost before purchasing tickets.
2.
Proposed Self-Developed Software Solution
This proposal aims to develop an AI-powered flight comparison system that calculates the “true cost” of air travel based on the user’s actual needs.
The system will include an Add-on Fee Database to store common extra fees from different airlines, such as baggage, seat selection, meals, and processing fees. These data can be collected from public airline information or manually maintained fee tables.
The system will also provide a True Cost Calculator. Users can enter their travel requirements, such as destination, date, checked baggage weight, carry-on needs, and seat preference. The system will then combine the base fare with required add-on fees and display the estimated final cost for each flight.
Finally, an AI-Based Recommendation Module will suggest the most suitable option. For example, it may recommend buying a baggage bundle, purchasing add-ons separately, or choosing a full-service airline if it provides better value.
3.
Expected Improvements
The proposed system can make flight comparison more transparent and efficient. Users can avoid manually checking multiple airline websites and reduce the risk of unexpected extra costs during checkout. By showing the estimated final price and providing recommendations, the system helps users make better travel decisions. Overall, this software can make flight selection more accurate, convenient, and user-friendly.
